About Us

Mufflers For Less has been family‑owned and operated since 1987. Over the decades we’ve built our reputation on honesty, integrity and quality workmanship at an affordable price.

From everyday drivers to custom show cars, our specialists craft exhaust systems with custom bends and treat every vehicle with the same care and attention. Whether you need a new muffler, brakes or simply want a second opinion, you can count on us for honest, friendly service.
